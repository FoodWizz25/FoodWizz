Find answers to common questions about FoodWIZZ

How do I add a new product to the inventory?

Simply navigate to the Products section in your dashboard, click "Add New Product," and fill in the required information including name, category, quantity, and expiration date. The system will automatically generate a unique product ID.

How do I search for a product in the inventory?

Use the search bar at the top of your inventory dashboard. You can search by product name, category, or product ID. The system also supports advanced filters for more specific searches.

How do I update the quantity of an existing product?

Click on the product you want to update, then click the "Edit" button. You can modify the quantity, update expiration dates, or change any other product details. Changes are saved automatically.

Can I generate an inventory report?

Yes! Go to the Reports section where you can generate various types of reports including inventory levels, consumption patterns, waste reports, and financial summaries. Reports can be exported as PDF or Excel files.

What do I do if I have a duplicate product?

The system automatically detects potential duplicates when adding new products. If you find duplicates, you can merge them using the "Merge Products" feature in the product management section.
